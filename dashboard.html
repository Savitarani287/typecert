Tips to improve
⌨️
Master the home row
ASDF and JKL; are your foundation. Practice keeping fingers on these keys and reaching out for others without looking.
👀
Look ahead, not at your hands
Train your eyes to stay on the text, always reading 2-3 words ahead of what you're typing. This prevents hesitation.
🎯
Accuracy before speed
Don't rush. A 90% accurate 40 WPM is better than a 70% accurate 60 WPM in net score. Slow down to speed up.
🔥
Daily 15-minute sessions
15 focused minutes every day beats 2 hours once a week. Consistency builds muscle memory faster than intensity.
